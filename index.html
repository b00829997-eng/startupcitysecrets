What is CitySecrets?

We are a young start-up determined to transform the way people think about tourism in Italian cities of art.

Over the years, as tour operators, we have seen hundreds of people and families visit the artworks in our cities as if they were a checklist.

We believe that this approach to tourism does not allow people to fully enjoy the atmosphere and treasures that cities of art have to offer: to solve this problem, we founded CitySecrets.

CitySecrets organizes treasure hunts suitable for all age groups in Italian cities of art.

As you try to solve the puzzles that will bring you closer to discovering the treasure, you will explore the city and savor its atmosphere outside the classic "tourist route": you will discover the most important monuments and interact with them to find the next clue, but you will also be taken to authentic areas of the city that only locals know about.
